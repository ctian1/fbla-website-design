Our Story

About ten years ago, three young men from the Bay Area moved to the great city of Salt Lake City. Their experience with a Family Entertainment Center back home was extraordinary, and they were sad to learn that no such facility existed in their new home. Thus, the three young men decided to work on a project to create something similar to what they had loved so much back in the Bay Area. Today, their project has become known as the SLCCC.


Here at the SLCCC, we are committed to helping our members stay fit, stay healthy, and have fun. We have the facilities and activites that will satisfy the needs of the whole family. Whether you are looking for a place to work out, to play sports, or to send your kids to after school, we are happy to help you.
